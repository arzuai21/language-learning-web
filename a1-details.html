Structure: I am / You are / He is / She is / It is / We are / They are

Example: I am a student. / They are friends.

Sample Sentences

  • I am a teacher.
  • You are very kind.
  • He is at school.

How to Use "To Be" in Sentences

You can use the verb "to be" with nouns, adjectives or prepositional phrases.

"To be" will change the form depending on the subject. And it will come just after subject.

  • I am an engineer. (noun)
  • You are pretty. (adjective)
  • She is at home. (prepositional phrase)
